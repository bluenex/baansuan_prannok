Baan Suan Prannok

Apartment | 894 Thanon Prannok, Baan Changlhor, Bangkok

Baan Suan Prannok Apartment provides utmost convenience which make you fill like home. Its location gives not only safety environment but also easy access to public transportation which allows you to go sightseeing along Chao Phra Ya river, Bangkok's landmarks, shopping center and more

History

Baan Suan Prannok Apartment was created by the 6 daugthers and son of Khun Sawasd and Khun Lamiead Mavai. The children grew up and separated to work and live in different places. However, the memory of living happily together with their parents in their garden home in Thonburi area, has always been on their mind. This lead to the decision to together establish Baan Suan Prannnok Apartment, with 40 rooms, which would allow people who have to work, study and live away from home to be able to live happily in a garden environment that is as similar to the memory of the garden house in Thonburi area as much as possible.
